    Tesla Investory Day

    Elon Musk, has revealed that the company’s highly anticipated Master Plan 3 will be unveiled at the 2023 Investor Day in March. The central theme of the plan will be focused on “scaling to extreme size,” with additional details yet to be disclosed, Master Plans serve as the company’s blueprint, detailing its global objectives and strategies to advance towards its mission accelerate the world’s transition to sustainable energy.

    Master Plan 3, the path to a fully sustainable energy future for Earth will be presented on March 1.

    The future is bright! pic.twitter.com/11ug0LRlbD

    — Elon Musk (@elonmusk) February 8, 2023

    The Tesla CEO has previously released two Master Plans for the company. The first one was published in 2006 and outlined a step-by-step plan to build a sports car, followed by more affordable cars, and also provide clean energy options, then come the second Master Plan, released in 2016, focused on creating solar roofs with battery storage, expanding Tesla’s electric vehicle product line, developing self-driving technology, and allowing Tesla cars to make money for their owners when not in use.

    Now it is time to find out what master plan 3 has up its sleeves! One thing is that excitement is guaranteed, there have been rumors about the company announcing a new Gigafactory location, potentially in Canada or Mexico, but this has not been confirmed.

    Our investors will have the chance to see our cutting-edge production line and converse with our leadership team about long-term expansion plans, the next-generation platform, capital allocation, and other subjects, said Tesla.

    Main Tesla subjects will be scaling to extreme size, which is needed to shift humanity away from fossil fuels, and AI.

    But I will also Include sections about SpaceX, Tesla and The Boring Company.

    — Elon Musk (@elonmusk) March 21, 2022

    Tesla’s 2023 Investor Day is not just about discussing long-term plans! With Elon Musk and the leadership team on hand to answer questions, the event is sure to be filled with entertaining moments, witty remarks, and maybe even a few surprises, so whether you’re a die-hard Tesla fan or just looking for a good moment, be sure to tune in to the live stream on March 1st and see what the future of sustainable energy has in store!
